Main Characters

Characters appearing in the Dusttale AU.

Dust!Sans (Murder!Sans)

The main character. Obsessed with stopping the human, he manually killed all the monsters to gather LV.

Phantom Papyrus

A phantom of Papyrus that only Sans can see. He is the embodiment of Sans's regret and madness, often encouraging Sans to continue the killing.
